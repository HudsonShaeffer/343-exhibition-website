About Me

Hello! My name is Anato Finnstark and I am a French native and long time artist. I have worked professionally as an illustrator and concept-artist for Unit Image, Wizard of the coast, Dark Horse Studio, National Library of France, Materia Collective, and lots more!

My inspirations contributing towards my art work has been heavily driven from my love of fantasy movies, shows, and video games. More particularly from some of my favorite movies/shows of the Lord of the Rings, Games of Thrones, Dune, and The Witcher. However, my occasional video game sessions playing Dark souls Three and Bloodborne contributed towards my imaginative creations.
